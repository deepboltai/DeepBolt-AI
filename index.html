About Us

Welcome to Fantasy11

Fantasy11 is India's premier fantasy sports platform, bringing you the most exciting and rewarding fantasy gaming experience. Since our inception in 2020, we have been committed to providing a fair, transparent, and entertaining platform for sports enthusiasts across the country. Our platform has grown to serve over 10 million users nationwide, making us one of the most trusted names in fantasy sports.

Founded by a team of passionate sports enthusiasts and technology experts, Fantasy11 was born out of a simple vision: to create a platform where every sports fan could experience the thrill of being a team owner, strategist, and winner. We believe that sports are not just about watching; they're about participating, strategizing, and celebrating victories together.

Our journey began with cricket, the heartbeat of Indian sports culture. From the electrifying IPL matches to international tournaments, we've been there for every boundary, every wicket, and every moment of glory. Today, we've expanded our offerings to include football, basketball, kabaddi, and many other sports that capture the imagination of Indian sports fans.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the fantasy sports industry in India by creating an inclusive, skill-based gaming platform that celebrates the passion for sports while ensuring complete fairness and transparency in all our operations. We strive to build a community where knowledge, strategy, and passion for sports are rewarded fairly and consistently.

We are committed to promoting responsible gaming and ensuring that our platform remains a source of entertainment and skill development. Our mission extends beyond just providing a gaming platform; we aim to educate users about sports statistics, player performances, and strategic thinking that can enhance their overall sports knowledge and appreciation.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and innovative fantasy sports platform in India, where millions of sports fans can come together to showcase their knowledge, compete fairly, and celebrate their love for sports. We envision a future where Fantasy11 is synonymous with fair play, innovation, and community building in the fantasy sports ecosystem.

Our vision includes expanding our reach to rural areas of India, making fantasy sports accessible to every sports enthusiast regardless of their location or background. We believe that talent and passion for sports exist everywhere, and our platform should be the bridge that connects these passionate fans to the exciting world of fantasy sports.

  • • Daily fantasy contests with exciting prize pools ranging from hundreds to lakhs
  • • Secure and instant payment processing with multiple payment options

Our platform features over 500 different contest formats, ensuring that there's something for every type of player. Whether you're a casual fan looking for low-stakes fun or a serious strategist aiming for the big prizes, Fantasy11 has the perfect contest for you. We regularly introduce new game formats and features based on user feedback and emerging trends in sports.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our operations. Every contest, every result, and every transaction is conducted with complete integrity. Our users can trust that their experience on Fantasy11 is fair, secure, and transparent.

We continuously innovate to enhance user experience and stay ahead of industry trends. Our development team works tirelessly to introduce new features, improve existing functionalities, and ensure that Fantasy11 remains at the forefront of fantasy sports technology.

Responsibility

We promote responsible gaming and provide tools and resources to help users maintain a healthy relationship with fantasy sports. We are committed to preventing problem gambling and ensuring that our platform remains a source of entertainment and skill development.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from user experience design to customer service. Our commitment to excellence drives us to continuously improve and deliver the best possible experience to our users.

Our Technology

Fantasy11 is built on cutting-edge technology infrastructure that ensures reliability, security, and scalability. Our platform can handle millions of concurrent users during peak events like IPL finals or World Cup matches. We use advanced algorithms for contest creation, result calculation, and fraud detection.

Our mobile application is optimized for Indian network conditions and works seamlessly on both high-end smartphones and budget devices. We understand that our users come from diverse backgrounds and use various devices, so we've ensured that Fantasy11 provides a consistent and smooth experience across all platforms.

Security is paramount in our technology stack. We employ bank-grade encryption, multi-factor authentication, and continuous monitoring to protect user data and financial transactions. Our systems are regularly audited by third-party security experts to ensure the highest levels of protection.

Fantasy11 is powered by a diverse team of over 200 professionals, including software engineers, data scientists, sports analysts, customer service representatives, and business experts. Our team members come from various backgrounds but share a common passion for sports and technology.

Our sports analysis team includes former cricketers, football players, and sports journalists who bring deep insights into player performances and match dynamics. This expertise helps us create more engaging contests and provide valuable insights to our users.
